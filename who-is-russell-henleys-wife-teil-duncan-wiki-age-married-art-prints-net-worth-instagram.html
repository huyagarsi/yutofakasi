Teil Duncan Biography – Teil Duncan Wiki

Teil Duncan is an American artist and painter from Columbus, Georgia. She is the wife of Russell Henley, an American professional golfer who competes on the PGA Tour. Henley won the 2010 Haskins Award as the most outstanding collegiate golfer and played in the U.S. Open. Henley made his PGA Tour debut at the Sony Open in Hawaii in January 2013, he won the PGA Tour title in his rookie year.

Duncan currently works as a full-time painter in Charleston, South Carolina. She was born and raised in Columbus, Georgia, United States. She developed a love for art at a very young age. Duncan attended Auburn University in Auburn, Alabama, where she earned her Bachelor’s degree in Art.

According to her website, Duncan is inspired by light, movement, surprising color combinations, social interaction, and pattern, her goal is to infuse these elements and form abstracted, pixilated compositions. No matter the subject, she strives to portray a balance of realism combined with loose abstraction, causing one to compliment the other. She resides in Charleston, South Carolina, United States.
