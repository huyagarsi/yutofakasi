Catherine’s Early Life, Family, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education And Religion

Catherine Reitman grew up in a family of filmmakers.

Her father, Ivan Reitman, is a famous director and producer who made iconic comedies such as Ghostbusters, Stripes, and Twins.

Her mother, Geneviève Robert, is a French-Canadian actress who appeared in some of her husband’s films.

Notably, her brother, Jason Reitman, is an Oscar-nominated director and producer who made films such as Juno, Up in the Air, and Thank You for Smoking.

Her other brother, Daniel Reitman, is an attorney.

Catherine Reitman has a mixed ethnicity of Jewish, Slovak, and French-Canadian.

She attended Cate School, a boarding school in California, and then graduated from the University of Southern California with a degree in English and Creative Writing.

She is not religious, but she celebrates Jewish holidays with her family.

Reitman’s Professional Career

Catherine Reitman started her acting career at a young age, appearing in some of her father’s films, such as Ghostbusters II, Kindergarten Cop, and Dave.

Some of her notable appearances include Knocked Up, I Love You, Man, How I Met Your Mother, Black-ish, and It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia.

In 2017, she launched her own comedy series, Workin’ Moms, which she created, wrote, executive produced, and starred in.

The show is inspired by her own experiences as a working mother and features a diverse cast of women who face various issues, such as postpartum depression, infidelity, career changes, and identity crises.

Notably, the show has received positive reviews from critics and audiences, and has been praised for its honesty, humor, and representation.

The show has also been picked up by Netflix, making it available to a global audience.

Besides acting and writing, Catherine Reitman is also a producer and a podcaster.

She has produced some of her own projects, such as Workin’ Moms and Missi and Zach Might Bang, as well as other films and shows, such as Friends with Benefits and Breakin’ It Down with Catherine Reitman.

She is also the host of the podcast Missi and Zach Might Bang, where she and her co-host Zach Selwyn interview celebrities and improvise songs.
