When Kim Kardashian appeared on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on April 22nd, 2024, fans might not have expected the conversation to cover Donald Trump. Other possible topics might have been the awe-inspiring lifestyle Kim leads thanks to being a member of the rich Kardashian-Jenner family, her family life, or her clothing business Skims.

However, huge Kim Kardashian fans know that she once spent quite a bit of time meeting with Donald Trump when he was in the White House. Since several years have passed since that initial meeting and Trump was in court for his hush money trial in April 2024, it's hard not to be curious about what Kim thinks about him now.

In this article, we'll take a look at how Kim Kardashian and Donald Trump got along before her Jimmy Kimmel interview, along with what she said about the former President during this episode.

Inside Kim Kardashian And Donald Trump's Relationship Before Her Jimmy Kimmel Live! Comments

While Kim Kardashian talked about Donald Trump on Jimmy Kimmel Live!, there is a lot more to the story of their relationship. It's important to note that things started out well between the two... but their relationship was a negative one before her comments on Kimmel. According to Business Insider, Kim Kardashian and Donald Trump talked about criminal justice reform at the White House in 2018 and 2020. In 2018, Trump tweeted, "Great meeting with @KimKardashian today, talked about prison reform and sentencing."

Business Insider reported that Donald Trump was then angry when Kim Kardashian asked him to release someone from prison, he said, according to the book "Tired Of Winning" by Jonathan Karl:

"Hell no. You voted for Biden and now you come asking me for a favor?"

While Trump's claim that Kim is "overrated" proves that he isn't a fan of hers, there is even more proof that the former President doesn't like this member of the Kardashian family.

According to OK! Magazine, Trump answered questions about various celebrities in 2023 on the podcast Full Send. Trump called Kim "disingenuous," which might not be surprising, given the other things that he said about her in the past.

As she shared in a 2023 interview with Time Magazine, Kim has cared about criminal justice for a long time and has tried to help get people released from prison. Since this was a cause close to her heart, Kim must have been happy to talk to the then President about it. Since Donald Trump began insulting Kim Kardashian a few years after they discussed criminal justice in 2018, that must have been upsetting for her. She was particularly passionate about helping Alice Marie Johnson who was given a life sentence for a conspiracy related to substances.

Donald Trump insulted Kim Kardashian's pregnant body in 2013. According to Us Weekly, he appeared on HLN's Showbiz Tonight and said, “She’s gotten a little bit large." Kim was pregnant with North West, her first child.

What Did Kim Kardashian Say About Donald Trump On Jimmy Kimmel Live?

When Kim Kardashian appeared on Jimmy Kimmel Live! on April 22nd, 2024, the conversation turned to Donald Trump. Given the pair's history, it makes sense that the political figure would come up.

Kim said:

“I don’t think he likes me very much. But… I think he did amazing stuff with prison reform and let a lot of people out and signed an amazing bill with the First Step act and that’s what I’ll focus on.”
