Everything You Need To Know About Netflix Back To 15: Release Date, Cast, Synopsis

Back to 15 is a Brazilian coming-of-age television created by Janaina Tokitaka. It was based on Bruna Vieira's novel of the same name. The teen series was co-directed by Vivi Jundi and Dainara Toffoli. The series was premiered on 25 February 2022, exclusively on Netflix. Maisa Silva, Camila Queiroz, Amanda Azevedo, Mariana Rios, Klara Castanho, Yana Sardenberg, Joo Guilherme, Bruno Montaleone, Gabriel Stauffer, and others star in the teen drama.

De Volta Aos 15 is the Netflix description for the series. The title's story revolves around a 30-year-old lady named Anitaand her accidental discovery with a time traveler. Anita becomes a 15-year-old girl when she wakes up and tries to address difficulties in other people's lives.

Maisa, the main character in this series, had big aspirations when she was younger. Her 30s, on the other hand, turned out to be the total opposite of what she had anticipated. As she approached her 30s, her teenage ambitions of leaving her hometown, traveling and meeting new people vanished. Anita, the main character, is shown attending a wedding in the show's trailer, and she is questioned if her life is going according to her hopes and goals. Then she transforms into a 15-year-old and seeks to right past wrongs.
