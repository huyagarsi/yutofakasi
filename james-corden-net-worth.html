What is the Salary/Income of James Corden in 2024?

In 2024, James Corden‘s annual salary is around $9 million, primarily earned through his role as the host of The Late Late Show.

How James Corden Built His Wealth

James started his journey in the entertainment world with small roles and theatre gigs. His big break came with the British sitcom Gavin & Stacey, which he co-wrote and starred in. This success opened many doors, leading to lucrative roles in film and TV.

His most notable gig? Hosting The Late Late Show. This role not only brought him international fame but also significantly boosted his earnings.

The show’s popularity, especially with segments like Carpool Karaoke, made him a household name and a high earner in late-night TV.
