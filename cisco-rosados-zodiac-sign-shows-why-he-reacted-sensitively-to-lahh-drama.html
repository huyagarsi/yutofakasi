Cisco Rosados zodiac sign shows why he reacted sensitively to LAHH drama Tue 15 February 202

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  733 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Cisco Rosado ended up getting involved in LAHH drama this season, with Peter Gunz and Rich Dollaz. But the Valentine’s Day 2022 episode saw him show the love and apologise for his wrongdoing.

Award-winning rapper Peter and his co-star Cisco had argued after it was revealed that while traveling, Peter’s then-estranged wife Amina Buddafly crashed at the music executive’s home.

The pair had been close friends before the beef, but managed to settle the drama by the finale episode of VH1 Family Reunion: Love and Hip Hop Edition. But there could be an influence for Cisco’s apologies – his zodiac sign.

Cisco Rosado’s LAHH drama

Cisco ended up having some beef over the past LAHH season, mainly over Peter Gunz‘ ex-wife Amina Buddafly. When she stayed at the rapper’s home, Peter wasn’t happy about it, despite his close friend insisting nothing happened.

Other issues between the two Creep Squad members also involved Cisco stopping Peter from receiving his money. Peter then took to Instagram to reveal he’s ready to take Cisco on for a boxing match. He wrote:

Looks good on the gram but without legs and cardio you will get the sh*t beat out of you. Don’t let my age fool you lil bro, boxing in the ring and fighting on Love & Hip Hop is two different things!! No security, no @monascottyoung and no @stefateaser to talk us down!!

He later posted a photo of Cisco with his son, and wrote in the caption:

@iamtarawallace said don’t forget @cisco_rosado is godfather to our son Gunner. I’m training to knock him out and she sends me this.. I think she hates me too.. @celebrityboxing1 what’s the odds on me?

However, Cisco settled things with a public apology to Peter during the February 14th episode. It also comes after Peter demanded an apology and his money from Cisco, which ended up with them having a physical altercation.

His zodiac sign explains apology

Cisco’s apology to those he feuded with actually saw Rich Dollaz shake his hand in tears. Rich told his friend: “We can’t help you if we don’t know what’s going on with you, right? You’ve got to come to us as your brothers.”

His public sorry – on the mic – involved Cisco saying he “probably irritated half of y’all“, before he went on to say that he feels he rejects the concept of family.

Citing that the trio are the ‘Creep Squad’, Rich Dollaz and Peter Gunz encouraged their buddy to “open up” in future.

The traits of someone with a Cancer sign are labelled as: nurturing, sensitive, compassionate, self-protective, security-seeking and offering, and loving. So it’s clear Cisco is a softie at heart who just wanted to stop arguing with his friends.
